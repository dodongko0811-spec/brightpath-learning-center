## Practice small separations

Short visits, brief goodbyes, and predictable pickup times help children learn that school is safe and that parents always return.

## Build the morning routine early

When children know what to expect before they leave home, the day starts with less stress and more confidence.

## Talk about the day ahead

Simple previews of who they will see, what they will do, and when they will come home can make a big difference.
