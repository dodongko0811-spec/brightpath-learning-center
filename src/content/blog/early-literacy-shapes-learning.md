## Reading begins with rhythm

Children build confidence when reading feels familiar. Short stories, repeated phrases, and shared routines help the brain connect language with meaning.

## Everyday moments count

Labels, signs, menus, and book titles all give children opportunities to notice letters and sounds in the world around them.

## Celebrate the attempt

The goal is not perfect reading right away. The goal is curiosity, confidence, and a calm habit of returning to books each day.
