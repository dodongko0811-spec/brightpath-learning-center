## Look for structure and warmth

Strong programs balance kindness with clear routines. Children should feel welcomed, but also know how the day works.

## Ask about communication

Families deserve regular updates, honest conversations, and a team that responds thoughtfully when questions come up.

## Notice how children move through the space

The right center should feel calm, organized, and child-friendly without feeling overly rigid or sterile.
